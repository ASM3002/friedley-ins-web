Proud to be serving the local community over 70 years

A family owned insurance agency

Friedley Insurance Agency was started in the Village of New Washington, Ohio in 1953 by Rolland W. Friedley. The agency incorporated in 1977 and became known as Friedley & Co. Agency, Inc.

Mr. Friedley's daughter, Blythe Friedley, began working in the agency in 1989. Upon her father's death in 1996 she became the agency's current President and Owner. She was joined by her youngest son, Todd McDougal, in 2002.

The agency first occupied a one room office in the back of the local barbershop in addition to various other locations within the village's business district during its first 50 years. In September of 2004, it acquired its own office building, a large beautiful turn of the century Victorian home, located in the center of town. This building now houses a staff of 3 employees eager to service your insurance needs.

This 2nd and 3rd generation family owned and operated business has been proud to serve the insurance needs of the community of New Washington and the surrounding area for over 50 years.
